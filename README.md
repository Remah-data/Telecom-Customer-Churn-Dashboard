#  Telecom Customer Churn Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project analyzes **customer churn** in the telecom industry using **Power BI**.  
The dashboard helps understand customer behavior and identify the main factors that lead customers to leave the service.

The focus is on delivering **clear insights** through a **clean and professional dashboard** designed for business decision-making.

---

## Business Objective
- Understand why customers churn
- Identify high-risk customer segments
- Support data-driven retention strategies

---

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- DAX
- Data Visualization & Analysis

---

## Dashboard Highlights
- **KPIs**
  - Total Customers
  - Churned Customers
  - Churn Rate (%)
  - Average Monthly Charges

- **Analysis**
  - Churn by Contract Type
  - Churn by Internet Service
  - Churn vs. Customer Tenure
  - Churn by Gender
  - Churn by Payment Method

- **Interactive Filters**
  - Contract Type
  - Internet Service
  - Gender

---

## Key Insights
- Month-to-month contracts have the highest churn rate
- Fiber optic customers churn more than other service types
- New customers are more likely to leave early
- Electronic check payment method is linked to higher churn

---

## Business Recommendations
- Promote long-term contracts
- Improve retention strategies for new customers
- Review pricing and service quality for fiber optic users
- Encourage stable payment methods

---
